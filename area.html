The Area

The Southern Highlands,
as we'd tell a friend

One of the best parts of staying here is everything just beyond the gate. Cool-climate wineries, historic villages, hatted restaurants and honest country pubs, dramatic waterfalls and bush trails — and the wildlife that wanders the property itself. We've gathered our favourites into four guides for you to use during your stay.
